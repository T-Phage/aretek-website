About Us

The rapid increasing population and disease burden in Africa coupled with the low socio-economic class of most Africans causes a huge unmet gap between quality healthcare delivery and healthcare needs in Africa. In line with our pursuit to ensure a healthier Africa, we at Aretek Pharma Limited believe that everyone is deserving of accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare products. We are passionate about improving patients and clients’ health and quality of life and we will leave no stone unturned to achieve that.

Our highly motivated, competent, and goal-oriented employees with excellent customer care are always willing to discharge their duties in line with the mission of the company without compromising on ethical standards. We shall continue to fulfil our Corporate Social Responsibilities as well as support the growth of our dynamic pharmaceutical industry.

Our symbol

Consist of the letter A representing ARETEK and the pharmaceutical cross symbol. ARETEK is coined from the Greek word Arete, which means excellence of any kind and bound up with the notion of fulfilment of purpose and Tek, from technology. We are going to use cutting-edge technology to achieve our purpose of improving the state of healthcare in Africa and we are so passionate about it.
