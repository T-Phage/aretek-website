About Us

The rapid increasing population and disease burden in Africa coupled with the low socio-economic class of most Africans causes a huge unmet gap between quality healthcare delivery and healthcare needs in Africa. In line with our pursuit to ensure a healthier Africa, we at Aretek Pharma Limited believe that everyone is deserving of accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare products. We are passionate about improving patients and clientsâ€™ health and quality of life and we will leave no stone unturned to achieve that.

Our highly motivated, competent, and goal-oriented employees with excellent customer care are always willing to discharge their duties in line with the mission of the company without compromising on ethical standards. We shall continue to fulfil our Corporate Social Responsibilities as well as support the growth of our dynamic pharmaceutical industry.

Our symbol

Consist of the letter A representing ARETEK and the pharmaceutical cross symbol. ARETEK is coined from the Greek word Arete, which means excellence of any kind and bound up with the notion of fulfilment of purpose and Tek, from technology. We are going to use cutting-edge technology to achieve our purpose of improving the state of healthcare in Africa and we are so passionate about it.

Divisions

We produce safe, effective and affordable pharmaceuticals according to Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP) and environmental protection measures that meets regulatory and statutory standards.

Our retail outlets are managed by qualified, dedicated, and experienced pharmacists and medical assistants. Our retail pharmacies offer a wide range of pharmaceutical and consumer products such as baby foods and cosmetics. Our aim is to provide convenience and to bring quality and affordable products close to our customers.

To be able to achieve our purpose of improving healthcare in Africa, we are committed to ensuring that quality and affordable pharmaceuticals reach every part of Africa. We, therefore, are building a robust medium to enable that. We partner with both local and international companies as well as hospitals to ensure that quality products are available whenever and wherever needed.
